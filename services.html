FAQs
How early should I book?

Peak season (Oct-Mar) fills six months out. Reach out as soon as venues are confirmed.

Do you travel internationally?

Yes. Destination weddings include travel fee + accommodation. Cones are packed per airline guidelines.

What products are used?

Only organic Rajasthani henna, lavender/tea tree essential oils, sugar, lemon, and distilled water.

Can you host group workshops?

Private henna bars, corporate wellness, and creative workshops are available on weekdays.
